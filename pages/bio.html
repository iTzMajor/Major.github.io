My Biography

Maor "Major" Dahari was born at October 18th 2001 in Israel.
Currently Major is a college student working to get his diploma as a Practical Enginner while his working on creating his brand online.
On 2014 Maor lunched his first channel on Youtube under the name "iTzMajor", It was around 2016 that "Major" decided to take down the channel.
The Youtube channel was daily active and it was Gaming content that was uploaded in English,
later on in 2018 Major decided to start the channel once more only this time it'll be in Hebrew.
Major Lunched his first Discord server "Major's Space" for both Hebrew and English languages and closed the gates last year on June 24th 2020.
During 2020 Major joined Twitch, a platform made special for live streaming and focused on that.
Major stream on Twitch every Monday, Tuesday and Thursday at 7PM IST (=Israel Standard Time).
Currently on Twitch Major streams different categories but mainly stream Dead by Daylight while every stream is in English.
In order to create a strong Community Major created "MajorLUL" a Discord server dedicated for his viewers and supports on Twitch and other platforms
which has been working aside Disboard in order to showcase and premote the server on the biggest servers list site on the internet.
On the start of 2021 Major joined TikTok in order of expanding it's brand which have proven to be useful and crucial for his community.
Along side of that, Major created an account on Twitter to post live updates and improve his bond with his viewers.
8 Years ago on 2013 Major joined the largest gaming network in the industry made by Valve named Steam under the username "iTzMajor".
on April 2021 Major lunched a Steam group for his viewers to communicate and join games along side Major during community.
